My journey as a Designer

Lessons learnt (part 1)

I started out as a social media manager and a digital marketing intern and so far i can say i got a couple of transferrable skills that made my transition into UI/UX smooth but notwithstanding the challenges, Here are a list of things i learnt

  • No knowledge is a waste
  • You cannot jump certain steps so don't be in a hurry
  • You are not an island, talk to people who know better than You
  • Learning never ends, so don't put a timing to it
  • Be open to good critics, it will help you learn faster
